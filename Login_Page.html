Announcement

Notice:

  1. Student payment is processed in accordance with the University's tuition and miscellaneous fee schedule.
  2. A two-stage fee structure is adopted for super senior students. The first-stage payment of the miscellaneous fee should be made within the payment deadline, Feb 6, 2023. Another payment notice for the second-stage payment of the credit fee is issued after the course add deadline, Feb 21, 2023 (base date for credit fee calculation) (The national defense and military education training and physical education course is charged for 2 credits). If the “total credits” of the super senior student reaches 10 (inclusive) credits, full tuition and miscellaneous fees of the original department should be paid.
  3. For super senior students, students undertaking Bachelor of Continuing Education degrees, or students who adopt credit fees calculation, the actual credit fees are calculated based on the credits of the course selected on the course add deadline, Feb 21, 2023. For students who have not processed course-drop before the course add deadline, the course credit fee is not refundable (those who have not paid are still required to pay the credit fee). Notices are issued to pay or refund after the course add and drop.
  4. If you have any questions, please contact the Office of Finance (04-24517250#2058).

 

For additional course selection information, please refer to 2023 Spring Semester Course Selection Calendar (https://ppt.cc/fGL2tx).
